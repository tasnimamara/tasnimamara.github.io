Projects and Experiences:

FireFighter Bot

Project Overview

I built this in highschool for my grade 12 engineering class. The FireFighter bot is an autonomous robot designed to navigate a maze and locate the fire source, which in our case was a candle. Once the fire source is detected by the IR sensors, the robot activates the fan to blow out the flame. The project involves integrating various sensors, such as flame sensors and ultrasonic sensors, to enable the robot to detect walls and the fire source effectively.

Components Used

  • PIC16f18875 microcontroller
  • 3 PCB that my teammate and I designed using Traxmaker pro
  • 2 ultrasonic sensors
  • IR flame sensors
  • Motors
  • 12v Fan
  • Wood, Threaded lamp rods, wires
  • Power Supply

Skills

  • PCB Design and fabrication
  • Soldering
  • Microcontroller programming (PIC16f18875)
  • Sensor integration (ultrasonic and flame (IR) sensors)
  • Autonomous navigation algorithms
  • Mechanical design and assembly
  • Hardware troubleshooting and circuit analysis
  • Team collaboration and project management
