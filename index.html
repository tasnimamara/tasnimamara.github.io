What is Nanotechnology Engineering?

Nanotechnology engineering is basically just a mix of electrical engineering, chemical engineering, mechanical/material engineering and chemistry (a truly interdisiplenary field XD), but at its core, nanotechnology engineering is about solving big problems by working at the smallest scale. Nano engineers act as a bridge between the nanoscale and real world innovations. Whether you need stronger, lighter materials for mechanical systems, faster and more efficient electronic components, smarter chemical processes, or entirely new materials designed from the ground up, my skills will deliver that next-generation advantage.
